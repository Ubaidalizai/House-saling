Refinance Rate Help

Looking for current refinance rates in San Francisco, CA? Here’s how to use our refinance rate tool to find competitive interest rates.

Loan purpose

You can search for today’s interest rates for purchasing a home or for refinance mortgage rates.

Zip code

Enter your ZIP code for the most accurate refinance rate results. Interest rates can vary from state to state.

Purchase price

This helps calculate the loan‑to‑value ratio for your loan, which helps determine your interest rate.

Down payment

This amount will also affect your interest rates. Most home loans require a down payment of at least 3%.

Credit score

Also known as a FICO score, a higher credit score will help you qualify for a lower mortgage rate.

VA Loans

Looking for a VA loan? Check the “Military/Veteran” above to get current refinance rates for VA loans.

FHA loans

Our refinance rate tool automatically includes FHA loans. You can filter results to only show FHA loans.

Advanced options

Use the advanced options to add details like income and debts for an even more accurate rate quote..
